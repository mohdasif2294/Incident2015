Musical Band Alchimix/Atma

Date : 06 March
Venue : Student Activity Centre (SAC) at 19:00 hrs

Prepare to be taken into a world of classical fusion sounds, the combination of violin and drumspace, supported by beat box and electronic environments. With Theo Poizat, Roland Carbety, Julien Moretto and Adam Smaali, this French and Moroccan lineup of hand pan, percussion, beat box, mouth harp, guembri and violin is sure to keep you hooked.
