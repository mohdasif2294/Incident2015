War of DJs

Spin the disc (DJ MIXING COMPETITION)

It’s time to get excited! The 1st audio mixing competition is kicking into gear! Spin, roll and turn your discs to make some of the funkiest beats anyone would crave for. Make the crowd go wild with your mindblowing creativity.

Incident NITK is giving emerging DJ's out there the chance to put their mix where their mouth is & step up their rep.

Date : March 06
Venue : Beach

Armed with a set of turntables DJ's will battle it out amidst the wind,waves and the clamoring crowd at NITK's own private beach. Watch as they prove that with every beat & every mix, they're making more than a song - they're creating an atmosphere.

It’s never been easy, but now its upto you!

Rules and Regulations:

  1. This is an individual event with maximum time limit of 15mins.
  2. 2 Pioneer 850 Players and a DJM 700 Mixer will be provided at the venue.
  3. The deck will be provided by the college. Only headphones are allowed to be brought by the participants.
  4. Participants should not use their own studio work remixes
  5. Participants should not play with same CDs simultaneously
  6. Use of Controllers is not allowed.
  7. The Participant in allowed to jam in any track of their choice.
  8. The mixes will be kept with the judges until the announcement of the results.
  9. The submissions will have the copyrights and cannot be uploaded anywhere until the results.
  10. The Judges’ decision will be final and binding.

Suggestions

  • In case of any discrepancies, the final decision will rest upon the judges.
  • Participants have to post a non-stop mix tape of 10 tracks of your choice in any genre on a CD. Duration of the mixing on stage will be 20 minutes
  • 3 minutes for sound check and 15 minutes for playing. Any variation in time limit will lead to deduction of points.
  • All tracks should be untagged. If any tag is found in the mixing on the stage, it will lead to disqualification of the participant.
  • Any kind of abusive lyrics will also lead to disqualification of the participant.
  • You also need to make sure the venue has suitable DJing equipment available or you. If for example you can not get hold of vinyl equipment you need to make this clear on the poster/application form

Criteria for Judjing

  • Best Mixing
  • MC-ing
  • Crowd Response
  • Number of songs played
  • Talent round (looking for special skills in mixing).
