Raagalaya

Indian classical music is magnificent, sublime, oldest and complete form of music. We request the pioneers of rich Indian culture and tradition to represent the greatness of Indian minds and inspire us with their quest for knowledge and excellence. Incident 2015 invites everyone to join us and rejoice the everlasting ecstasy of divine music.

Date : 05 March
Venue : Pavillion

General Guidelines

  1. Maximum time allotted is 8+2 minutes.
  2. The event consists of only one round.
  3. Electronic tambura will be provided.
  4. Instrumentalists need to bring their own instruments.
  5. Vocalists and instrumentalists (both carnatic and hindustani) will be judged together.
  6. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Carnatic

  1. Participants are expected to perform madyamakala kritis.
  2. Accompaniment (only mridangam) will be provided.
  3. More weightage will be given to manodharma sangeetam(raga alapana, tanam, kalpana swarams and neraval). Participants are requested to choose wisely and plan accordingly in the stipulated time frame.

Hindustani

  1. Participants are requested to avoid vilambit laya compositions.
  2. Accompaniment (only tabla) will be provided.
  3. Improvisations will be given preference. Layakari and bhaavang will be entertained.

Note : solo percussions cannot be accommodated in the competition.
