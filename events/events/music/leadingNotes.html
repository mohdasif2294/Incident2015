Sing your favourite songs and make yourself as our favourite by flying us to the world of your idiosyncratic voice. The platform has been set to showcase your singing talent this March as Incident gets high on colour. Besides, use the very same to manifest your dexterity in Instruments.

Date : 05 March
Venue : Pavillion

Rules

  1. Number of participants per team:
    Vocals: 1 Vocalist + 1 Instrumentalist(optional)
    Instruments: 1 Instrumentalist
  2. Time Limit: 5+2 minutes of time on stage inclusive of set up and is bound face penalty on exceeding the time limit.
  3. The vocalist can choose to play the instrument himself/herself or use a pre-recorded or karaoke sequence.
  4. The keyboard can be played only in the piano mode.
  5. There is no limit on the number of songs to be performed.
  6. Judging will be based on vocal abilities- adherence to pitch, tempo and quality of voice and overall rendition.
  7. Four entries per college are permitted.
  8. Registrations will be accepted online or on the spot at the registration desk on the day of the event.
