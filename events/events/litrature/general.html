General Quiz

Does your GK often have people who know you often referring to you as an 'insufferable know-it all' or something similar? Incident invites you to put your repository of knowledge to the test with our General Quiz.

Date : 08th March
Venue : Main Seminar Hall (MSH) / ISTE Seminar Hall

Quiz Master: Navin Rajaram
About the Quiz Master: Navin is an alumnus of the Indian School of Business (ISB), Hyderabad where he majored in Marketing and Strategy. He has 9+ years' professional experience with stints at Avaya and Nokia. His interests include history, music and movies. Navin has been a finalist in many national quizzes and is a part of the research team for several prestigious quizzes

Rules

  1. Teams of maximum 3 members.
  2. Any number of teams from an institution is allowed.
  3. Participants must produce a valid ID card during registration.
  4. Cross college teams are allowed.
  5. A written prelims round and an 8 team on stage Final round.
  6. The type of question in Prelims are a mixture of audio, video, connect, simple direct questions.
  7. The Final round will contain infinite pounce, passing the questions, audience questions, etc.
