Thematic Dance Competition

“Dance is the hidden language of the soul of the body”
- Martha Graham

Dance from heart and live the lyrics. An opportunity to make your dance do the talking on stage, make it a treat to eyes of the audience and make them connect with their inner self.

Date : 07 March
Venue : Silver Jubilee Auditorium (SJA)

Rules

  1. It is a Group dance competition.
  2. Each team should have minimum of 4 members. There is no upper limit.
  3. No restrictions on number of teams per college.
  4. Styles should be strictly contemporary.
  5. Props can be used on stage . (Fire , Water , Smoke etc are not allowed)
  6. The participants must get their music track in a pen drive or a CD. Music must be submitted 2 hours prior to the event at the reporting desk.
  7. Length of the routine – 5-8 minutes(+2 ).
  8. Judging Criteria –Concept, Choreography ,Co-ordination, Stage usage and Formations, Costumes, Creativity, Confidence, Intensity and Presence, Musicality, Overall impact.
