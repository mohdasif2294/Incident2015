Duet Dance Competition

“It’s all about the chemistry”- A chance to display synchronization at every step, every spin, every lift you make on stage. Fly, groove in this sensual form of dance that’s all about the chemistry and being in your comfort zone.

Date : 06 March
Venue : Silver Jubilee Auditorium (SJA)

Rules

  1. Team size- 2
  2. No restrictions on number of teams per college.
  3. Participants are allowed to perform any style .
  4. Props can be used on stage . (Fire , Water , Smoke etc are not allowed)
  5. The participants must get their music track in a pen drive or a CD. Music must be submitted 2 hours prior to the event at the reporting desk.
  6. Length of the routine – 3-5 minutes(+2 ).
  7. Judging Criteria –Choreography ,Stage usage, Co-ordination, Costumes, Creativity, Confidence, Intensity and Presence, Musicality, Entertainment factor or crowd appeal.
