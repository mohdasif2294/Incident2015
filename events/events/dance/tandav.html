Tandav

“Artistry is admired because it looks ‘effortless’ after constant practice”
- Sudharani Raghupathy

Every state has their classical dance which is a mystic manifestation of the metaphysical elements. A chance to showcase these divine dance forms on stage and express your passion towards it in the land of Shiva, ‘Shiratakal’ or Surathkal.

Date : 06 March
Venue : Silver Jubilee Auditorium (SJA)

Rules

  1. It is a Classical Dance Competition.
  2. Each team should have minimum of 4 members. There is no upper limit.
  3. No restrictions on number of teams per college.
  4. Any classical, regional or folk dance piece is allowed.
  5. Props can be used on stage . (Fire , Water , Smoke etc are not allowed)
  6. The participants must get their music track in a pen drive or a CD. Music must be submitted 2 hours prior to the event at the reporting desk.
