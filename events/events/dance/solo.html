Solo Dance Competition

“If you can’t get rid of the skeleton in your closet, you’d best teach it to dance”
- George Bernard Shaw

No restriction on the form of dance, just your passion towards it. This Incident, bring I your A-game and Impress the judges with your skills.

Date : 06 March
Venue : Silver Jubilee Auditorium (SJA)

Rules

  1. Team size- 1 + Assistant if required.
  2. No restrictions on number of teams per college.
  3. Participant is allowed to perform any style.
  4. Props can be used on stage . (Fire , Water , Smoke etc are not allowed)
  5. The participants must get their music track in a pen drive or a CD. Music must be submitted 2 hours prior to the event at the reporting desk.
  6. Length of the routine – 2-4 minutes(+2 ).
  7. Judging Criteria –Choreography ,Stage usage, Costumes, Creativity, Confidence, Intensity and Presence, Musicality, Entertainment factor or crowd appeal.
