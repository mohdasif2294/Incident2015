Street Play

Away from the stage lights and curtain calls, view acting in its most raw, natural form. Be it socially, politically or artistically motivated, get inspired as teams perform and communicate to 'the man of the street' in an exuberant combination of drama, dance and dialogue.

Rules

  1. Number of participants (including the supporting and the guest actors) should not exceed 20.
  2. Time duration of the act should be 10-15 minutes.
  3. Teams will have to be flexible regarding the venue of the performance of the act as there will be no fixed venue. However, the venue will be notified in advance. Teams may have to perform at any part of the NITK campus.
  4. Teams will have to arrange their own costumes and props if they need so. Nothing will be provided from our end.
  5. Use of Microphone and Speakers is prohibited. However, the use of non-electrical musical instruments is allowed.
  6. Teams will be judged on the basis of THEME, COORDINATION, DIALOGUES, CREATIVITY, MESSAGE and ENTERTAINMENT QUOTIENT. Decision of jury will be final.
  7. The act should be either in Hindi or English. Use of any other language is prohibited.
