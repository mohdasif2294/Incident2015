Haute Couture

Do you think graduate colleges are filled with geeky nerds? We dare you to say that once you look at the sizzling hot divas and utterly charming dudes walk and strike a pose on the ramp this March at Incident’15. With outfits that will make you burn with envy, style that will take your breath away, music that will block every thought other than that of awe and lights that will dazzle you, Haute Couture brings a night of elegance, luxury and beauty that simply must not be missed!

From Greek to Diabolical, Hip-Hop to Punk, Zodiac to Bohemian, Bikers to Cowboys. Watch these modern day fashionistas scorch, sizzle and take your breath away. Dazzling lights, feet-thumping music, deadly divas and funky clothes! Need we say more?

Date : March 05
Venue : Student Activity Centre (SAC)

RULES

One entry per college.
The competition comprises of two rounds

Round one is based on:

  1. Theme – CLASSICS
  2. Classics is a general theme, each team has to come up with a subtheme that comes under that category. It should be clear to the judges how your theme falls under it.
  3. Time limit: 10-15 min (Including setup and performance)
  4. Number of participants on stage: 14 to 20
  5. Number of backstage people (Including make-up artists, hosts, etc.): 5 to 7.

Round two is based on:
  1. Theme selected open to your choice.
  2. Time limit - 10-15 min (Including setup and performance)
  3. Number of participants on stage - 14 to 20
  4. Number of backstage people (Including make-up artists, hosts, etc...): 5 to 7.

Any Choreography aides that may be potentially dangerous or destructive to property like fire, water etc... are strictly prohibited
DECISION OF JUDGES IS FINAL AND BINDING
Prizes to be won:
  • Team:
    • Winners
    • First runners-up
  • Individual:
    • Best design
    • Best walk
