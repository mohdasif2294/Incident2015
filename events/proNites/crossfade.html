Crossfade (Mighty Fools & DJ A Sen)

Date : 08 March
Venue : NITK Main Grounds

EDM Night- Awaken the party animal you. Let the electronic music take hold of you and keep dancing till your body is sore.

This year,SonicBoom presents the EDM night featuring DJ’s of two different genres. DJ Mightyfools, A brainchild of two Dutch DJ’s Jelly Keizer and Andy Samin. This duo has performed at international festivals and highly respected venues such as Tomorrowland, Creamfields, Mysteryland main stage in Chile/USA/NL, World DJ Festival South Korea main stage, Ushuaia Ibiza, Hard Rock Las Vegas, Avalon Hollywood, Webster Hall NYC, Heineken Music Hall Amsterdam, Pacha Sydney, Skygarden Bali and Cubic Club Macau.

DJ A Sen, one of the hottest talents of the country has gained popularity with his eccentric electronic tracks. He has officially remixed many Bollywood tracks and has been named the Best Bollywood DJ of 2014. His tracks have had international acclaim and have been played at clubs of various countries.
