Eastern Night (Coshish)

Date : 08 March
Venue : Student Activity Centre (SAC)

Eastern Nights- Catchy music and unforgettable melodies, Eastern Night promises you the performances of bands ranging from The Indian Ocean to Swarathma to Divine Raaga.

Incident 2015 in association with Coke Studio presents you Coshish, a progressive rock band. Get set to experience an amalgamation of progressive rock with Hindi drawing elements from Tool and Porcupine tree to Mohammad Rafi and Bhimsen Joshi. A night that will appease your endearment towards Eastern Rock.
