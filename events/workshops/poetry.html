Airplane Poetry

Date: 5th March 2015
Venue: Physics Seminar Hall

In June, 2014, 19 poets from Bangalore got together to answer one question - is poetry dead? The result was the Airplane Poetry Movement - A project to create a vibrant and energetic spoken word poetry culture among the youth. Spoken Word, as opposed to the written form, allows you to give life to everything you write and speak. How many of you thought you needed a perfect place, the perfect breeze and perfect mood to scribble down some rhyming stuff? We think you will only need the perfect team to get you started.
