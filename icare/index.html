i-Care

I-Care, the social event of Incident, was first organised in 2011 by an enthusiastic group of people who wished to inculcate humanitarian ideals among the student fraternity. Since its inception it has become bigger and better every year due to the commitment and dedication shown by an ever increasing number of altruistic volunteers.
We organise two major events, one being the I-Care Day and the other, Street Plays.
I-Care day is a full day event, where kids from orphanages spend a whole day in college engaged in various events organised. We aim on making it a fun cum learning experience by conducting a Sherlock Holmes event. It will be a treasure hunt based on clues which will lead to solving a mystery. This will take them to different parts of the campus where we will be explaining to them about the departments, functioning of CCC, ATM, etc. Lunch will be provided to them. Further into the day we have planned a T-Shirt painting workshop for them by the Artist Forum. The T-Shirts will be given to them at the end of the day along with mementos for participation.
The street plays on burning topics of the country are performed to bring in awareness among the students to make this society a better place to live. Last year, the play was about Women Harassment which received a wonderful response from the students as well as the media. This time we plan to work on Swachh Bharat, to create awareness about cleanliness. The street plays will be performed at the beach in the evening, during the fest.
Highlight of this I-Care will be a performance by the specially-abled kids from the Lions Club School, Surathkal. They will be performing during Promenade, the dance competition of the fest.
